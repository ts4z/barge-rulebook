# Nit Levels

*This section is new and may have errors.*

*Nit levels* provide a way of using *nit cards* to encourage action in
tournaments.

At the beginning of each level before the final table, the dealer will issue a
nit card to each player. When a player wins a pot, they return the nit card to
the dealer. Players still holding a nit card at the end of a level must pay an
ante of 5 big blinds for the first pot of the new level.

At the final table, players will once again receive a nit card. The last player
holding a nit card at the final table is eliminated and their chips are removed
from play.
