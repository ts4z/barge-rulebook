# Bomb Pot

In a *bomb pot*, all players post an ante pre-flop, and there is no pre-flop
betting.  Instead, the three-card flop is dealt and action commences with the
player to the left of the button.  The minimum bet size is the size of the
ante.  Otherwise, play is as normal.  A dealer button is still used and rotated
around the table to determine the player that will be first to act on each
betting round.

Bomb pots are common in no-limit [Texas Hold'em](texas-holdem.md) and
[Omaha](omaha-high-only.md) games.
A bomb pot NLHE tournament was played at [BARGE](https://www.barge.org/barge/)
[2021](https://www.barge.org/barge/2021/).

Bomb pots are common in cash games, but the details vary from room to room.
